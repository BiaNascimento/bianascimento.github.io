About Memories of Fireflies

Responsabilities: Level Designer, Producer

Check the game

Memories of Fireflies is a puzzle platformer game made by Smart Studio, a Brazilian indie studio that hired the company I was working on to provide consultancy and follow the game development.

On the game you play as a traumatized child that must overcome her fears with the help of a therapist, and trace your journey back to home.

I was responsible to follow the game development, organizing the team members demands and deadlines and, on a first moment, offering consultancy on level design and game design matters. After realizing that there wasn’t a studio member with enough knowledge on level design, I was also responsible for the game level design.

The major difficulties of this project included work on a team that I wasn’t part and with a low technical level, as they were all beginners in the game industry, besides being reticent with the received feedbacks. Deadlines were also a big challenge, as all the levels had to be designed within 15 days with little space for iteration.

But there was a greater win: the project was then backed by Microsoft for an Xbox porting that is currently under development.

Design Process

Onboarding

from meetings with the team I tried to understand their needs and how to achieve the objectives together.

Follow-up

I’ve followed the game development, always giving consultancy specially about the game and level design aspects of the game, but sometimes also giving advice about more technical process.

Level Design creation

to fulfill the studio needs I was responsible for the game level design, which was made on 2 weeks using Construct 2 as the prototype platform. The levels were implemented and tested as far as possible with a tight deadline.

Final version

The game was released on Steam with more than 15 levels.
